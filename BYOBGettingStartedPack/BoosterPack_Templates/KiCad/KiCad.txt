KiCad is an open source hardware design tool. It is free to use and getting very popular. You can learn more at http://www.kicad-pcb.org/

BoosterPack Templates are available within KiCad at "KiCad\share\template\ti-boosterpack40" and "KiCad\share\template\ti-boosterpack20"

They are also included in the BYOB Getting Started Pack.
